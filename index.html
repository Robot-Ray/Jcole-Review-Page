Review

The new J. Cole album, The Fall Off, delivers a focused and polished sound. Even from the point of view of a listener who is usually critical of J. Cole, this project feels thoughtful, confident, and well-produced.

Music critic Anthony Fantano discusses the high expectations around the album and why it stands out as one of Cole's most talked-about releases.

Watch Anthony Fantano's review

Highlights

This album has the feeling of a final statement: strong production, reflective lyrics, and the kind of careful tracklist sequencing that makes the whole project feel intentional from start to finish.

If you like album reviews, hip-hop discussion, and artist retrospectives, this release gives plenty to talk about.
